About This Project

Seated Martial Arts adapts martial arts principles to seated positions, offering a way to practice without needing to stand. These routines are easy to follow and can be done at a desk, at home, or wherever you’re sitting.

The focus is on maintaining flexibility and strength while also providing a way to manage stress and improve focus during your day. It’s a straightforward approach that fits into your routine without extra hassle.

Whether you’re new to martial arts or just exploring a different way to practice, Seated Martial Arts is a practical and flexible option for anyone.
