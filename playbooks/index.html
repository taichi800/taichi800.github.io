Our Martial Arts Offerings

Everyday Basics

Start here for practical, approachable martial arts routines that build physical readiness and confidence.

  • Health and Wellness: Stress reduction and physical readiness through Tai Chi and martial arts.
  • Self Defense: Practical modules for safety and confidence building.

Daily Motion

Accessible routines for light movement and personal expression, from functional exercises to community dances.

  • Practical Moves: Functional routines for everyday activities, blending light movement with practical skills.
  • Choreographed Arts: Light, visually engaging routines like guangchangwu for personal or community enjoyment.

Strength and Performance

Physically demanding routines for those seeking to build strength, stamina, and precision.

  • Fitness: Physically demanding routines for strength, stamina, and conditioning.
  • Performance: Advanced martial arts routines focused on skill development and precision.

Focus and Flow

Calming routines that center the mind and body, blending mindfulness and gentle movement.

  • Meditation: Breathing exercises and mindfulness for decision-making and focus.
  • Yoga Mat Series: Gentle movements integrating yoga and martial arts for constrained spaces.

Lifelong Practice

Archive of routines and forms: Explore martial arts at your own pace, with resources for all skill levels and goals.
