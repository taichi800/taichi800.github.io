Everyday Basics
Health and Wellness: Learn routines focused on improving physical comfort, mobility, and overall wellness. Set up to support relaxation.
Self-Defense: Explore practical methods for recognizing and responding to situations. Focus on safety and prevention for real-world applications.
Daily Motion
Practical Moves: Explore movements that can fit into your daily routines. These exercises are inspired by martial arts principles and aim to make everyday tasks feel more comfortable and efficient.
Day-to-Day: Try simple, adaptable exercises to help with posture, balance, and alignment in regular activities. Influenced by a variety of movement practices, this module supports regular movement with ease.
Strength and Performance
Fitness: Engage in exercises for building strength, endurance, and coordination. Adjust routines to match your preferences and abilities.
Performance: Structured routines and progression rankings designed to enhance precision, timing, and consistency in movements. Suitable for improving physical skills in structured ways.
Focus and Flow
Yoga/Yoga Mat: Combine yoga-inspired movements with martial arts principles. Includes yoga asanas done in sequences or individually. Seeks to explore movement within space constraints.
Meditation: Use breathing exercises in various contexts such as sitting, standing, and moving. Adapts focus as needed for practical use.
Lifelong Learning
Lifelong Learning: Martial arts encompass countless systems, styles, and routines, each with its own background and purpose. This space is for those who wish to continue practicing the routines they are already familiar with, regardless of style or origin. It offers a supportive framework for individuals to focus on their chosen practices at their own pace, with the flexibility to continue, adapt, or explore routines as they see fit.
