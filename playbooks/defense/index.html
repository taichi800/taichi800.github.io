About This Project

Self-defense is about learning practical ways to stay safe and handle unexpected situations calmly. Basic Self-Defense Tactics cover important areas like understanding your surroundings (situational awareness) and using straightforward techniques for striking and escaping when necessary. Each module focuses on practical approaches to help you stay safe.

Communication is a key part of readiness—expressing yourself clearly and assertively in difficult situations can make a big difference. We also include basic legal information to help you understand how to act within safe and appropriate boundaries. Additionally, there is an emphasis on prevention techniques and staying composed under pressure.

Whether you’re completely new to self-defense or want to build on what you already know, these tactics are designed to suit different lifestyles and physical needs. Learning to avoid risky situations and stay prepared is a valuable skill that benefits from regular practice.
